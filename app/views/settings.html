Direct Debit

What is a SEPA Direct Debit ?

The SEPA Direct Debit is the new european standard for bank direct debits. It is progressively replacing the French bank direct debit with RIB and debit authorisation.


How does the SEPA direct debit work?

Follow these steps :
1.Enter your IBAN and BIC in the following form.
2.Download the document 'Mandate for SEPA Direct Debit'. Fill in the downloaded form. Don't forget to sign it.

3.Scan and upload the signed mandate by clicking on 'Scan'
